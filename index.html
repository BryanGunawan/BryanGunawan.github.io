Inspiration

Bryan loves development, technology, and impact.

Believing in inclusion and diversity, Bryan had trained over 10,000 individuals for 10 years professionally and socially, performed in a few short movies, and influenced numerous communities. His work and community exchanges have brought him to a fortunate event to meet President Barack Obama in Jakarta Roundtable Meeting and in Obama Foundation Summit in 2017. Since 2012, Bryan founded his social entrepreneurship company in communication and leadership development. He gained trust and grew for Indonesia and his surroundings. He does not fear ambiguity, he embraces innovation and data analytics. He continues to be a versatilist in transforming clients and delivering impact that goes beyond the dollar value.
